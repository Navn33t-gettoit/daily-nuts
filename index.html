How to Play

Every day, you face three poker boards. Everyone plays the exact same ones.

Your goal: pick the two missing cards that create the absolute best possible hand for that board. In poker, this unbeatable hand is called "the nuts." You get three tries per board.

The levels

The boards get harder as you go: ♠ Gentle → ♠♠ Tricky → ♠♠♠ Devilish.
Solving just the first board keeps your daily streak alive. Solving all three is a clean sweep.

Beat the clock

The real challenge is speed — find the answers in the fastest time and let the group chat know. The clock only runs while you're playing (it pauses whenever you're reading results), and a slow time never costs you a win.

Feedback colors

🟩  Perfect. You found the nuts.
🟨  Right type of hand, but a higher one is possible.
🟥  Wrong. A completely different, stronger hand beats yours.

Any two cards that tie the best hand count as a win.
